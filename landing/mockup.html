AI in your sidebar, on any webpage.

Ask any model about the page you're reading — summarize, extract, translate, or just chat. Six providers, one keystroke, no context-switching.

Coming to the Chrome Web Store soon. Free and open source under the MIT license.

Why Aside

Built to disappear into your reading.

You're in the middle of an article, a research paper, an API doc, or a long email thread, and you want a model's take — now, without losing your place. Aside opens a slim sidebar on the page you're already on, already aware of what you're looking at.

Press Alt+A and ask. The response streams in token-by-token; you can cancel, edit, and ask again. Conversations are saved per-site so you can pick up where you left off.

One keystroke

Alt+A on any page. The sidebar slides in, already aware of what you're reading.

Six providers

Claude, Gemini, GPT-4o, Grok, Groq, and local Ollama. Switch in a click, no separate logins.

Page-aware

Summarize, extract key points, translate, find on page, or run a custom prompt. No copy-paste.

Privacy

Your keys, your machine, your data.

API keys live in Chrome's encrypted local storage and are sent only to the provider you chose, over HTTPS. There is no Aside server, no proxy, no analytics, no telemetry.

Page content is included only when you ask, trimmed to twelve thousand characters, and sent with that single request. Pick Ollama and nothing leaves your computer.

Open source, MIT licensed, free forever.
